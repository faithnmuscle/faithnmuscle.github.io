About the Coach

Hi, I'm Bernil

Free information is everywhere, but why invest in coaching with me?

Because I believe real results come from real knowledge and real experience - not from shortcuts, fad diets or copy-paste workout plans. I am an NVQ Level 4 Sports Massage Therapist, NVQ Level 4 Fitness Trainer and FOFATO Emergency First Responder.

I have worked in multiple gyms after dropping out of school during advanced level classes. I was blessed with the opportunity to train, meet and talk to many different and amazing people, serving as a strength and conditioning coach at my own school. I worked with bodybuilders, powerlifters, school and full-time athletes, complete beginners and patients under medical guidance - helping them improve strength, fitness and overall health step by step.

I started online coaching once I kept procrastinating this opportunity amid many of my client requests. The reason I did that was that I was not confident enough - I don't have many pictures of me training another. It's just me. But here I am, ready to help you reach your goals safely and realistically.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Quick answers about coaching, plans, payment and policies.

What's the difference between 1-on-1 coaching and a standalone plan?

Coaching (LKR 10,000/month) includes daily check-ins, workout and nutrition plans, tracking and ongoing adjustments on WhatsApp and video calls. Standalone workout, meal, athletes or rehab plans are one-time programs with the depth described on each card—without the full month-to-month coaching relationship.

How do I pay, and when does my program start?

After you submit the right Google Form, I'll reply on WhatsApp with payment instructions (e.g. bank transfer). Work begins only after full payment is confirmed. Submitting the form does not reserve a slot until payment is complete.

How long until I receive my plan?

Custom plans are usually delivered within 48–72 hours after payment is confirmed, depending on workload and how complete your questionnaire answers are.

Can I cancel or get a refund after my plan is ready?

Once a plan has been prepared, cancellation is not offered. See the Refund Policy below for details.

Can I share or resell my plan?

No. All material is for your personal use only. Copying, sharing, reselling or distributing plans without permission is not allowed.

I have an injury or medical condition—can I still sign up?

For rehab and flexibility work, get clearance from your doctor if the condition is serious or medical. Coaching and plans are educational and not a substitute for medical diagnosis or treatment.
